Who Are We and What Do We Do

Hill Bottom Properties specializes in a diverse portfolio of residential, commercial, and mixed-use real estate investments across Ethiopia. This includes luxury condominiums, townhomes, and apartments, as well as office buildings, retail centers, and industrial facilities. We work closely with our clients to identify the optimal property types that align with their investment goals and risk profiles.

As a vertically integrated real estate firm, we take a comprehensive approach to identifying, acquiring, and managing properties on behalf of our clients. This includes in-depth market research, financial modeling, property due diligence, hands-on asset management, and portfolio optimization strategies. Our team of seasoned real estate professionals partners closely with investors to build customized, high-performing real estate portfolios.

Navigating Ethiopia's evolving real estate regulations is a key priority for our firm. We have a deep understanding of national and local zoning laws, licensing requirements, tax obligations, and other compliance considerations. Our legal and operations teams work diligently to ensure all of our properties and transactions adhere to the latest rules and best practices.

Investments and Management

We cater to a range of investment time horizons, from short-term value-add projects to long-term, stabilized holdings. Many of our clients seek 5-10 year hold periods to capitalize on Ethiopia's robust economic growth and property appreciation. However, we also accommodate investors with more opportunistic, shorter-term strategies.

Absolutely. In addition to acquisition and investment services, we provide comprehensive property management for all assets under our care. This includes tenant screening and relations, rent collection, maintenance and repairs, and regular performance reporting. Our hands-on approach ensures the optimal performance and risk mitigation for our clients' properties.

Our rigorous due diligence process involves in-depth market research, financial analysis, site inspections, and regulatory reviews. We assess factors such as location, property condition, rental rates, operating expenses, and projected returns to determine the viability and fit for our clients' investment criteria.
