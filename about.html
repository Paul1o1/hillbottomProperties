Our Story

Hill Bottom Properties stands as a leading real estate company in Ethiopia. Established with a vision to provide exceptional living and commercial spaces, this dynamic firm has carved a niche for itself in the city's thriving property market.

The idea was first conceptualizes in 1991 during our service with the UNHCR in Uganda, Hill Bottom Recreation Center became a reality in 2007. Inspired by the Ethiopian song "Gara Sir New Betish," our center offers a wide range of sports and health facilities to the emerging middle class in Addis Ababa.

Over the years, we've expanded our services to meet the growing needs of the community. With the influx of younger residents, we've transformed Hill Bottom into a village of its own, featuring luxury middle-class apartments alongside top-notch health and sports facilities. Welcome to a world of well-being and modern living at Hill Bottom.

One of our company's flagship developments is the Hill Bottom Village Apartment, a prestigious village apartment that seamlessly blends modern architectural design with traditional Ethiopian aesthetics. This exclusive enclave offers well-appointed apartments, each meticulously crafted to provide residents with a luxurious and comfortable living experience. Amenities within the complex include lush landscaped gardens, a state-of-the-art fitness center, and a community clubhouse for social gatherings.

With a team of dedicated professionals, including architects, designers, and construction experts, Hill Bottom Properties ensures that every project is executed with meticulous attention to detail and adherence to industry best practices. The company's reputation for transparency, integrity, and exceptional customer service has earned it a loyal client base and a strong reputation within the Ethiopian real estate market.

As Addis Ababa continues to grow and evolve, our company remains at the forefront, shaping the city's skyline with innovative and sustainable developments that cater to the diverse needs of residents and businesses alike.
