About Hill Bottom Village Apartment

Hill Bottom Village Apartment is a prime address for residents in Addis Ababa seeking spacious apartments, exclusive amenities and a community lifestyle in a picturesque village setting. A luxury apartment and exceptional amentites that create an indulgent residential experience in a charming neighborhood environment.

Project Overview

A comfortable, neighborhood atmosphere where residents feel at home. The spaces gives off a sense of graceful luxury, characterized by clean lines, rich textures, and a harmonious blend of contemporary and classic elements. The brand's commitment to preserving the character of the site coupled with its attention to craftsmanship, lends an authentic and genuine appeal.

Functionality

Our design approach prioritizes creating opportunities for social connections, shared experiences, and a feeling of being part of a vibrant and enriching community. Its a Sanctuary where discerning individuals can enjoy a privileged lifestyle, surrounded by like-minded neighbors.
